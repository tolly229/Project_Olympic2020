Machine Learning

Machine Learning is an integral part to this project. Once historical Olympic games data is structured and formatted, Machine Learning helps predict outcomes from next summer's 2020 games in Japan.

Final Prediction

Our machine learning model predicts the United States to take home the most medals at the 2020 Olympic Games with 110. They are followed in the top 10 by Russia, China, Germany, Great Britain, France, Australia, Italy, Japan, and South Korea.

Of the nearly 200 countries to participate, we predict 74 will win at least one medal.
