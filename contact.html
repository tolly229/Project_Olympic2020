Data

We collected our data from Kaggle. It is is a historical dataset on the modern Olympic Games, including all the Games from Athens 1896 to Rio 201696 to Rio 2016. It included Country, Athlete, Host City, Event and Medal information.

About Our Team

Our team is comprised of 4 students enrolled at Vanderbilt University Data Analytics certificate program. We each have a passion for looking at data to draw conclusions. The goal for this project was to predict medal counts for the 2020 Summer Olympics.
